My Major


Pennsylvania State University, University Park, PA

B.S. Cybersecurity Analytics and Operations with minor in Security Risk Analysis

The Cybersecurity analytics and operations program here at Penn State educates students on the constantly growing concepts of cyber-defense and the primary analytical fundamentals of cybersecurity. I have engaged in the concepts of protection of computer systems, networks, programs, and data from attacks and unauthorized access. The Cybersecurity classes here have provided me with several hands-on experience opportunities in the form of labs to help me learn and grow my skills.

A smaller part of my degree is Security Risk Analysis (SRA). This program enhances students critical thinking and analysis skills to address a wide range of security and risk domains mainly related to national/homeland security. Cybersecurity and Security Risk Analysis go hand in hand, hence why I decided to pursue both.

I really enjoy the Intelligence aspect of both Cybersecurity and Security Risk Analysis and hope to work for the government after college. Working for the FBI or the CIA would be very cool.
