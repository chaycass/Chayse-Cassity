K-pop Music and Dance (KPMD)

KPMD is a club for students to come together and share their interest in K-Pop through dance and music discussion. We have two meetings per week: Tuesdays and Saturdays. Tuesday is more for just hanging out with friends, playing games, and listening to music while Saturdays is where we learn the chorus of a K-Pop song. We also do dance covers. We try out for a dance, and if put in get assigned a member to learn their part. We devote two practices per week to learn transitions and clean the dance in order to film and put it on YouTube.

I have been in this club for over two years now and can say it was the best decision I ever made at Penn State. I've made so many friends of different backgrounds and got back into dancing. This club keeps me active and has made my college experience fantastic.

KPMD has enabled me have leadership roles. I have been a coordinator, or leader, of a few of the dances we have done. This involves me to create transitions, assign members, run practices, and overall be the main voice of the dance. These opportunities have taught me how to better lead a group of people which I will be able to translate within my future job.
