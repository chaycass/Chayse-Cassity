Twitch


A fun website I like to go on in my free time is Twitch. Twitch is a platform where people can showcase their creativity streaming live videos. With these streams, fans can watch you play video games, talk, etc. while interacting with you in real time. You can also make money from Twitch once big enough. I have a few favorite streamers that I liked to watch at night. My favorite streamer is CaseOh.



Spotify


Spotify is a digital music platform that allows users to stream music and podcasts. There are millions of songs on Spotify, and it is free to use. There is also a Premium version that enables users to listen with no ads and download their favorite playlists to listen offline (no internet). I use Spotify every single day while im walking to class, doing work, dancing, etc. Spotify is also a great place to listen to Podcasts. Some of my favorite I like to listen to are Darknet Diaries, CyberWire Daily, and the All-In Podcast. Spotify is very easy to use and has become a personal favorite of mine.
